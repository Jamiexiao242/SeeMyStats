How to export your Apple Health data

  1. Open the Health app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
  3. Scroll down and tap "Export All Health Data"
  4. The export may take a few minutes depending on your data size
  5. Locate export.xml and transfer it to your computer via AirDrop, email, or another method
  6. Upload export.xml on this site
